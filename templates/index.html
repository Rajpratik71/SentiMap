ABOUT SENTIMAP

SentiMap is a web app that plots the sentiment of geotagged tweets in realtime.

The colors range between blue and red, which respresent positive and negative tweets, respectively.

You can pan and zoom around the map by clicking and dragging or scrolling with your mouse and examine the text of user's tweets by mousing-over them. You can also click on a tweet to reveal the most prominent news stories in the vicinity.
