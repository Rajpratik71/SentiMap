ABOUT SENTIMAP

SentiMap is a web app that is meant to explore the interface between people's moods and current events by plotting the sentiment of geotagged tweets in realtime and presenting location aware news stories for requested regions.

The dots on the map represent individual tweets whose sentiment is predicted via machine learning techniques and assigned a color, which range between blue and red, corresponding to positive and negative tweets, respectively.

You can control panning and zooming around the map by clicking and dragging or scrolling with your mouse. You can also examine the text of user's tweets by placing your cursor over them or you can click on them to reveal the most prominent news stories in the vicinity as well as the sentiment score (from -1 to 1).

Feel free to view or download the code on GitHub or send me comments via the contact button below.
